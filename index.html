Central Park Flower Valley, located in Sector 32-33, Sohna Road, Gurgaon, is a premium township offering a perfect blend of luxury, wellness, and nature. Strategically situated in the foothills of the Aravallis, it provides serene views while being well-connected to major roads and the upcoming Rajiv Chowk–Sohna Highway. The township offers diverse residential options including Cerise Floors, Flamingo Floors, Mikasa Plots, Fleur Villas, Aqua Front Towers, and The Room, catering to different preferences and budgets. Aqua Front Towers feature spacious 3/4 BHK high-end apartments with customizable ultra-luxurious specifications, while The Room offers plush air-conditioned suites with nature views. Fleur Villas provide ultra-luxury villas with individual gardens, parking, and access to upscale amenities such as fitness centres, swimming pools, and landscaped parks. Residents enjoy 45 wellness features including yoga sessions, reflexology walks, spa treatments, naturopathy centers, laughter therapy, and meditation rooms. Sports enthusiasts have access to state-of-the-art gyms, all-weather swimming pools, and various sports academies, while children can enjoy a 2-acre amusement park with an aqua park. Dining and leisure options include Al-Capone multi-cuisine restaurant, exotic foot spa, and an organic café & salad bar. Security and convenience are ensured with 24/7 security, concierge, and housekeeping services. The township emphasizes green living with meticulously landscaped gardens, parks, and eco-friendly initiatives, making it a serene, luxurious, and sustainable choice for prospective homeowners.
